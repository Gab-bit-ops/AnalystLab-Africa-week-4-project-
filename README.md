# 🏬 Superstore Sales Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
This project tracks revenue, profit, and customer performance across regions, categories, and products to support data-driven business decisions. Built and visualized using **Power BI**, covering sales data from 2014 to 2017.

**Internship:** Analyst Lab Africa

---

## 🛠️ Tool Used
- Power BI — Data modeling, querying & visualization

---

## 📊 Dashboard Overview
- Total Customers — 793
- Total Revenue — 2.30M
- Total Profit — 286.40K
- Total Orders — 5K
- Profit Margin — 0.12


## 💡 Key Findings
- 🌍 **West region leads in profit** at 108K, followed closely by East at 92K — South and Central trail behind
- 📈 **Profit trends fluctuate by region over time**, with the West showing the strongest growth toward 2017
- 💻 **Technology drives the most sales** at 836.15K, followed by Furniture and Office Supplies
- 📦 **Phones and Chairs are top-selling sub-categories**, each generating 0.33M in sales
- 👤 **Customer SM-20320 is the top spender**, contributing significantly above other customers
- ✂️ **Copiers generate the highest profit** among sub-categories at 56K, while Fasteners and Machines fall at the bottom
